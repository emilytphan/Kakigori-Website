About Us

At Kakigori Dessert Café, we believe there's always room for dessert! Founded in 2020, our café brings together the warmth and sweetness of grandma’s kitchen with a modern twist. Our founder, who has a passion for baking since childhood, traveled abroad to share his love for desserts. From our signature Mango & Sticky Rice Soft Serve to refreshing Bingsu, each treat is crafted with the finest ingredients. Join us for a delightful experience that’s sure to bring a smile to your face!
